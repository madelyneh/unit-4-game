Crystal Guessing Game

You are given a random number at the beginning of each game.

Each crystal below will also be assigned a random number each game. By clicking a crystal you will be adding that amount to your total score.

To win the game you much use those four crystals to get your total score to match the random number.

You will not know what numbers are assigned to each crystal till you click them.

Click any crystal to begin.

Good luck!
